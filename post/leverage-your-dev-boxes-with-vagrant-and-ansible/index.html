Supercharge your developement VMs with vagrant and ansible

Share

Vagrant is defacto standard when it comes to managing your developement machines. It allows you to spin a multi-VM developement environement in a second. For more details….VagrantUP.

Vagrant isn’t a provisioning tool, but leverage the existing one, such as puppet and ansible. Ansible is the new one in the playground. The learning curve is pretty steep, you only write your playbook in YAML, and it is devOps friendly. Give it a try and clone my example project vagrant lamp ansible and get started.
